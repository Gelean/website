The Sons of Lanarchy

The Sons of Lanarchy are a close knit group of people born and raised in Southern California. Most of us attended South High School together where our shared passions for gaming and other nerdy activities provided the bedrock of a foundation that would stand the test of time. Although we are more spread out now, we enjoy gaming with one another to this day.
