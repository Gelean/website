The History of the Sons of Lanarchy

The Sons of Lanarchy are a close knit group of people born and raised in Southern California. Most of them attended South High School together where their shared passions for gaming and other nerdy activities provided the bedrock of a foundation that would stand the test of time. Although they are more spread out now, they enjoy gaming with one another to this day.
